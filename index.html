FAQ

Common questions.

Where does the property data come from?

PropPull uses the RentCast API (140M+ US property records sourced from county assessors and MLS feeds), FEMA's National Flood Hazard Layer for flood zones, and RentCast's listing database for active listing agent and broker info.

How does ZipForms auto-fill work?

PropPull's sidebar runs alongside ZipForms Plus. When you click "Fill ZipForms," the extension finds matching form fields on the page and fills them with your property data. Fields that can't be matched are flagged so you can fill them manually.

How many lookups do I get for free?

The free tier gives you ~16 property lookups per month. FEMA flood zone lookups are unlimited and free.

Does my agent profile persist?

Yes. Your name, license, brokerage, and preferred escrow are saved locally in Chrome and auto-fill buyer's agent fields on every new transaction.

What if a field is wrong or missing?

Every field is editable in the sidebar. If data is missing from the API, you can fill it in manually. Editable fields have a dashed border.

Is my data stored anywhere?

All data is stored locally in your browser. Property lookups and your agent profile never leave your machine. The only external calls are to RentCast and FEMA for data retrieval.
